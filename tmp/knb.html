05 QUALITY SOURCED LUMBER

We carefully select top-grade lumber, guaranteeing the finest and most durable kitchen cabinets. To meet this requirement, we have implemented a rigorous inspection process to assure that only first-class products arrive at your households.

.

06 PRO TOUCH TECHNIQUES

Expert finishing methods are required to influence first impressions and assure a lasting finish that is easy to maintain. Our pro-touch technique creates a durable, simple-to-clean cabinet that will hold its excellence for years.
.

07 DOVETAIL CONSTRUCTION

The dovetail construction process involves plywood side panels to perfectly interlock with a solid wood face frame through corresponding dovetail grooves. This highly resilient technique ensures that our cabinet lines are strong, secure, and long-lasting.
.

08 ANTI-WARP STRUCTURES

The anti-warp component involves the use of plywood panels comprised of rigid reinforcement beams. By applying the beams with a dovetail construction technique to secure all components, we provide this standard to guarantee a strong, reliable build that it’s resistant to distortion.

09 SOLID BACK BUILDS

For superior structural endurance, we construct the back of a cabinet from one piece of solid half-inch plywood. This feature transforms our cabinets to be more stable and durable.

10 FLUSH FIT FRAMES

All together for a cabinet to be a high-end quality product, a cabinet must have a flush inner frame that provides an appealing appearance while giving added dimension to the internal area. K&B’s cabinet lines display prime craftsmanship with their flat surface top and bottom interior planes, which result in practical and pragmatic use.

11 FINISHED INTERIORS

Well-inspected and finished interiors are another significant element of our quality crafted cabinets as it points clear evidence of quality and attention to detail. K&B’s enhanced treatment techniques create a delicate and durable design that is free of bubbling, peeling, and chipping.

12 SOFT CLOSING ACTION

Another great characteristic of HQ qualification is the use of compact hinges with the built-in, soft-close mechanism. K&B utilizes the smooth-running capability and soft closing action of DTC’s tandem plus self-closing hinges, providing an impeccably gentle and fluid cabinet function.

13 FLUID FULL EXTENSION

Cabinets built with smooth full-extension glides ensure an enhanced running action that maximizes its storage to its full capacity and guarantees HQ eligibility. K&B Cabinets uses DTC’s tandem plus runners and soft-closing technology to precisely utilizing space whilst preventing damage and depreciation.
